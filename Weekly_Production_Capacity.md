## Key Success Factors

1. **Pre-staged Materials**
   - Raw aluminum tube cut to length before the week starts
   - Hardware pre-kitted

2. **Focused Workflow**
   - One machine type per day (minimizes setup time)
   - Helper handles grinding, hole drilling, and packaging

3. **Co-Packer Partnership (Recommended)**
   - Josh manufactures components during his week
   - Co-packer stocks popular sizes and handles same-day shipping
   - This model allows Josh to only work ~1 week per month

4. **Batch Production**
   - Build in batches of 4–6 units of the same configuration
   - Reduces changeover time significantly
