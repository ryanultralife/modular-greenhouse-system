Engineered to take the weather

Standard straight-run builds are engineered for 130 mph winds and 6 feet of snow, and backed by a 10-year guarantee. The modular system is patented (US 10,426,102).

Custom multi-section layouts (L, T, X, or extended runs) are reviewed by an engineer before the wind and snow ratings are confirmed for your specific build — we'll handle that when you request a quote.

Questions

How fast can I add to my greenhouse?

Each 4-foot section uses a foldable hinge design and goes up in about two hours — no special tools or contractor needed.

What shapes can I build?

Straight runs, L-shapes, T-shapes, and X/cross layouts — all from the same modular sections, in 4-foot increments.

How tough are they?

Standard straight-run kits are engineered for 130 mph winds and 6 feet of snow, with a 10-year guarantee. Custom layouts get an engineering review.

How do I get a price?
